At AfriToys, we create durable, authentic, African learning solutions that foster creativity, innovation and literacy in children.

About Us

Numbers Board

What is a Numbers Board? Numbers Boards are an excellent addition to kindergarten number sense practice. They are a great way to show students the many different ways to represent a number.

Kenyan Flag Puzzle

A 5 piece wooden puzzle that teaches children the various colours and elements of the Kenyan flag. We are looking to foster patriotism and appreciation of the Kenyan and African culture.

Colored Clock Puzzle

A colourful wooden clock board made from Mvule wood; which is known for its durability and authenticity. It has 12 pieces that fit into matching slots and introduces children to time. It also teaches them different colors and shapes. This product has a lid option that fits in the different pieces well. It can be covered and put away neatly by the children as they tidy up their toys.

Puzzle Clock

A rustic wooden clock puzzle board made from Mvule wood; which is known for its durability and authentic feel. It has 12 puzzle pieces that intricately fit into one another. This product has a lid option that can be used to cover and put away the puzzle board neatly.

Our toys provide the opportunity for:


Hands on Learning

By engaging their senses and manipulating objects, children develop a deeper understanding of various subjects, fostering curiosity, experimentation, and a love for learning.

Role-Play and Imagination

Toys like dolls, action figures, or playsets enable children to engage in role-playing and imaginative play. Through these activities, children can create narratives, develop characters, and explore different roles and scenarios.

Open-Ended Play

Toys that encourage open-ended play, such as building blocks or art supplies, allow children to explore their imagination and creativity. These toys have no defined outcome, giving children the freedom to experiment, problem-solve, and express themselves in unique ways.
