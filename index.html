We create durable, authentic, African learning solutions for children.

At AfriToys, we create durable, authentic, African learning solutions that foster creativity, innovation and literacy in children.

As a parent of four, energetic children, we have seen firsthand the impact of blending play with education, our handmade, durable, sets of toys provide an opportunity for children to learn as they play.

About Us

Number Board

What is a number board? Number boards are an excellent addition to kindergarten number sense practice. They are a great way to show students the many different ways to represent a number.

Flag of Kenya

Our flag of Kenya toy allows children to get familiar with interacting with our nation's flag!

Colored Puzzle Clock

A fine crafted soft wooden puzzle offers a hand on introduction to how to tell time. While solving the puzzles, the children develop problem solving skills, object recognition and memory building skills.

Puzzle Clock

This helps help children learn basic concepts of time telling, colour recognition and number counting.

Our toys provide the opportunity for:


Hands on Learning

By engaging their senses and manipulating objects, children develop a deeper understanding of various subjects, fostering curiosity, experimentation, and a love for learning.

Role-Play and Imagination

Toys like dolls, action figures, or playsets enable children to engage in role-playing and imaginative play. Through these activities, children can create narratives, develop characters, and explore different roles and scenarios.

Open-Ended Play

Toys that encourage open-ended play, such as building blocks or art supplies, allow children to explore their imagination and creativity. These toys have no defined outcome, giving children the freedom to experiment, problem-solve, and express themselves in unique ways.
